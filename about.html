About Us

Our passion is to make rain jackets that will last you a lifetime - without hurting your wallet.

They are designed for a range of different outdoor activities and weather types - ensuring you are dry and comfortable whatever adventure you go on.

Please, let us know if you need help finding just the right jacket for you, or if you have any questions or feedback. We would love to hear from you!
